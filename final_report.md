**Final Report – Delhi Weather Analysis 2025**
**Executive Summary**
This report presents an analytical review of Delhi’s 2025 temperature and environmental data using 52,560 hourly observations.
**Key Insights**
Peak temperature observed in May.
Lowest temperature recorded in January.
Clear upward trend from winter to summer.
AQI levels elevated during colder months.
Strong seasonal climatic pattern detected.
**Conclusion**
Delhi’s climate in 2025 demonstrates predictable seasonal variation with extreme heat during peak summer and declining air quality during winter months.
