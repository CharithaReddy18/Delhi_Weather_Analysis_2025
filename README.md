**Delhi Weather Analysis 2025**
📌 **Project Overview**
This project analyzes the temperature patterns and environmental conditions of Delhi for the year 2025. The analysis was conducted from the perspective of a Senior Data Analyst in a weather forecasting team.

📊 **Dataset Information**
52,560 hourly observations
16 weather-related parameters
Temperature, Humidity, Pressure, Wind Speed, AQI, PM2.5, PM10, CO, NO2, etc.

🔎 **Objectives**
Analyze temperature patterns across 2025
Identify hottest and coldest periods
Study seasonal trends
Examine relationship between temperature and air quality
Present findings through a dashboard-style summary

📈 **Key Findings**
May recorded the highest average temperature.
January was the coldest month.
Strong seasonal trend observed across the year.
AQI levels were higher during colder months.
Clear environmental variation across seasons.
